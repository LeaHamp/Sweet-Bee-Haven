About Beekeeping in Oulu

Discover the unique challenges and rewards of beekeeping in Northern Finland

Why Beekeeping Matters in Northern Finland

Beekeeping in Oulu presents unique opportunities and challenges due to our northern location. Our short but intense summers, with their long daylight hours, create perfect conditions for diverse honey production. Local bees help pollinate both wild plants and agricultural crops, contributing to the region's biodiversity.

Seasonal Beekeeping Cycle

  • Spring (Kevät): Hive inspection and cleaning after winter
  • Summer (Kesä): Peak honey production during the midnight sun
  • Autumn (Syksy): Honey harvest and winter preparation
  • Winter (Talvi): Protecting hives during the cold months

Local Flora and Honey Types

Our bees collect nectar from:

  • Lingonberry and Blueberry flowers
  • Fireweed (Maitohorsma)
  • Dandelions (Voikukka)
  • White Clover (Valkoapila)
  • Raspberry flowers (Vadelma)
